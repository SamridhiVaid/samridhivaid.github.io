## Design Context

### Users
ML engineers, researchers, recruiters, and hiring managers visiting samridhivaid.com to assess Samridhi's expertise and work. They scan quickly for credibility signals (roles, impact metrics, publications) and may dig deeper into projects and research.

### Brand Personality
Capable, clear, grounded. A senior ML engineer who lets the work speak for itself.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Tone:** Clean and professional with warmth from typography and subtle color accents
- **Theme:** Light, warm off-white background with stone tones. Serif headings (Newsreader) paired with clean sans-serif body (Source Sans 3)
- **Color:** Restrained multi-accent palette (amber, teal, indigo, rose, emerald, violet) used sparingly as section indicators, not as decoration
- **No em dashes (---):** Use periods or commas for sentence breaks instead
- **Anti-references:** Flashy gradients, dark mode gamer aesthetics, overly decorated portfolios, generic AI-generated designs

### Design Principles
1. **Substance over style** - Every visual element should serve comprehension, not decoration
2. **Scannable hierarchy** - Recruiters get the story in 10 seconds; peers can drill into details
3. **Quiet confidence** - Let achievements and metrics speak; avoid overselling with visuals
4. **Typographic clarity** - Newsreader serif for personality, Source Sans for readability. No font gimmicks
5. **Restrained color** - Color as wayfinding (section dots, hover states), not as ornamentation
